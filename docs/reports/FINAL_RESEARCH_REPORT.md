# Final Research Report

This report is the assembly point for final claims. Until a final holdout protocol is frozen and executed, this document should remain conservative.

## Structure

1. Problem statement.
2. Data sources and contracts.
3. Feature and target definitions.
4. Model registry.
5. Validation protocol.
6. Predictive results.
7. Economic policy results.
8. Limitations.
9. Reproducibility appendix.

## Claim Template

Each final claim should include:

- model and policy IDs;
- symbol and horizon;
- exact train, validation, test, and holdout dates;
- baseline;
- delta metric and uncertainty;
- artifact path;
- status label.

## Current Status

The documentation architecture is ready. Final claim text should be filled only after the corresponding artifacts are selected and audited.
